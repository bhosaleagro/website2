Our Farming Philosophy

The Khillari Cattle Advantage

Our herd of indigenous Khillari cattle are more than just livestock - they're an integral part of our organic ecosystem. Known for their hardiness and disease resistance, these native breeds:

  • Provide high-quality manure for our natural fertilizers
  • Help maintain soil health through natural grazing patterns
  • Require fewer resources than commercial breeds
  • Preserve valuable genetic diversity

Jivamrut: Nature's Fertilizer

We prepare Jivamrut, a traditional bio-fertilizer, using ingredients from our farm:

  • Fresh cow dung and urine from our Khillari cattle
  • Jaggery and pulses grown on our farm
  • Hand-ground soil from our termite mounds
  • Water from our natural wells

This potent mixture enhances soil fertility naturally, promoting healthy plant growth without chemical inputs.
